---
title: Keybind Audit
---

Recommended baseline:

```ini
BACKUP_MENU_Key=F10
BACKUP_MENU_ModifierKey=None
```

- F4 — RPH console
- F11 — EUP Menu
- Left Shift — initiate traffic stop
- E — interact
- B — Ultimate Backup
- F10 — vanilla backup

Record every future key before adding the next plugin.
